About Job Tracker with Alerts

A smart application that helps you stay on top of job opportunities, tailored to your preferences.

Streamlining Your Job Search

One match at a time, tailored to your preferences.

Core Features

  • 1. Job Scraping: Automatically fetch job postings from websites like Indeed or LinkedIn.
  • 2. Database Integration: Store job postings and user preferences securely in a database.
  • 3. API Endpoints: Manage user accounts, preferences, and view saved job listings via a powerful API.
  • 4. Email Notifications: Get alerted when jobs matching your criteria are found.
  • 5. Scheduler: Automatically update job postings and notify users with task scheduling.

Optional Add-Ons

  • Secure user authentication using OAuth2 or JWT.
  • An admin dashboard to monitor and manage jobs, users, and preferences.
  • Advanced filtering and pagination to browse jobs by salary, location, and type.
  • Real-time WebSocket notifications for new job matches.
  • Cloud deployment for easy access anywhere.
